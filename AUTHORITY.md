# Authority Declaration — Master License Suite

## Human Authority

The Master License Suite is governed by explicit human authority.

All licensing terms, enforcement mechanisms, and canonical locks
are defined, approved, and maintained by Human Authority.

Automation, tooling, or downstream systems may not modify,
reinterpret, or supersede these terms.

## Scope of Authority

Human Authority is responsible for:
- Approving license changes
- Declaring canonical releases
- Locking enforcement boundaries
- Binding governance to downstream artifacts

## Non-Delegation

No automated system has authority to alter licensing terms
or enforcement constraints defined in this repository.

Human Authority is final.
